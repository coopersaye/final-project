History of Video Games

Welcome to the History of Video Games! This website explores the evolution of video games across three pivotal decades that shaped the gaming industry as we know it today.

Navigate through the decades to learn about the iconic games, consoles, and technological advancements that defined each era:

  • 1980s: The golden age of arcade games and the rise of home consoles
  • 1990s: The transition to 3D graphics and the console wars
  • 2000s: Online gaming, mobile games, and HD graphics

Each decade brought unique innovations and unforgettable gaming experiences that continue to influence modern game development.
